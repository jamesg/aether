Aether

Intelligent Plant Environment Monitor for Gardeners and Vegetable Growers

  • Home

Aether is a plant environment monitoring system. It monitors vital attributes of your plant's environment, to ensure that your plants have the best conditions in which to thrive.

Components

  • Base station
  • Wireless sensor
  • Web application
  • Knowledge base

Web Application

Aether's web application can be accessed from your computer, tablet or smartphone.

Planning

Aether gives you the tools to plan your plant's entire lifecycle, from sowing to harvest.

Sensors

Aether records the air temperature and soil moisture content to give you well-founded advice and warn you of conditions that may damage your plants.

Knowledge Base

Aether is supplied with a knowledge base detailing the ideal growing conditions of over two popular plant varieties. The knowledge base makes sure that Aether will only give advice relevant to the varieties you choose to grow, freeing you from the worry of different plant's requirements and giving you more time to get on with the gardening.

Web Application

Aether's web application gives you access to a wealth of information about your plants.

Multiple Devices

Access the web interface from your computer, tablet or smartphone. Aether is built with modern web standards, so it works with any modern web browser.

Locally Hosted

Aether is hosted on your own home network. An Internet connection is only required for knowledge base and software updates.

Plant Lifecycle

Aether can monitor several environments at once. When you first start the system, you will be asked to enter a list of locations for plants; these could be your windowsill, a coldframe, a South-facing wall or a vegetable patch - whatever suits your garden.

Phases

Hardware

The complete Aether system incorporates a base station and one or more wireless sensors. You place the wireless sensors next to a batch of plants you want to monitor, then assign the sensor to the plant's record using the web interface. Details of the plant will be displayed on the sensor's LCD screen, so you know it's set up correctly.

Expandable

You can monitor any range of environments by adding more sensors. Sensors have a typical range of 100m to the base station; plenty to cover a large plot, or a plot some distance from your house.

Knowledge Base

Aether has knowledge of over two varieties of plant, so it can provide detailed advice on how to care for each and every one of your plants. Moisture and temperature warnings will only be issued if they would damage the particular plant being grown.

Weather Forecasts

As well as keeping track of the local temperature, Aether can use an online weather forecast to warn you of harmful weather events several days in advance. You'll never be caught out by a late frost again! Aether can also predict droughts and will tell you when to give your plants extra water.

Care Calendar

Aether knows the best time to sow, plant out and harvest all the varieties in the knowledge base. It can suggest new varieties to sow each month and tell you how long you can expect to wait before cropping.
